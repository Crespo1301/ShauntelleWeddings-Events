About

A personal approach to planning with warmth, care, and beautifully intentional detail.

Shauntelle Weddings & Events is built on the belief that meaningful celebrations should feel personal, elevated, and genuinely attainable.

Meet Shauntelle

The creative heart behind the celebration.

Hi, I’m Shauntelle, the creative heart behind Shauntelle Weddings & Events. I am a part time wedding and event coordinator and designer with a passion for turning meaningful moments into beautifully curated experiences.

I was raised in Shasta County, and my journey into event design is deeply personal. Growing up in foster care taught me the importance of celebrating life’s milestones, especially the ones that make you feel seen, valued, and loved. Those experiences shaped my desire to create beautiful, memorable events that feel attainable and not overwhelming.

I believe everyone deserves a special day that reflects their story, regardless of budget. That belief is the foundation of my business. To help make high quality design more accessible, I proudly provide my full decor inventory at no additional cost to my clients when they use my services. This allows couples and families to enjoy an elevated, cohesive look while keeping services budget friendly and stress free.

What clients can expect

Thoughtful support from first idea to final detail.

Intentional Design

From custom decor and tablescapes to florals, balloons, and full event styling, every detail is chosen to feel cohesive and meaningful.

Seamless Coordination

Shauntelle can assist with venue selection, vendor management, timelines, food presentation, and the moving pieces that help an event flow beautifully.

Accessible Elegance

Full decor inventory is included with services to help create an elevated look while keeping the experience more approachable and budget conscious.

Why I do this

Because celebrations matter.

Milestones were not always guaranteed, which gave me a deep appreciation for the moments that bring people together. Those experiences shaped my heart for this work and inspired me to create events that feel full of love, joy, and connection.

I love what I do and it shows in my work. I always go above and beyond for my clients because I want them to feel supported and excited, not stressed or overwhelmed by cost. For me, this is not just about decor or timelines. It is about honoring stories, celebrating milestones, and creating a space where people feel proud of the moments they are making.

This work is personal. Every event I design reflects my commitment to making people feel special, cared for, and celebrated, because everyone deserves that.
