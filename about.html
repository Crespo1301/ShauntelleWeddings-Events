About

A personal approach to planning rooted in care, beauty, and connection.

Meet the creative heart behind Shauntelle Weddings & Events and the story that shapes each celebration.

Hi, I’m Shauntelle

I am the creative heart behind Shauntelle Weddings & Events, and a part time wedding and event coordinator and designer with a passion for turning meaningful moments into beautifully curated experiences.

I was raised in Shasta County, and my journey into event design is deeply personal. Growing up in foster care taught me the importance of celebrating life’s milestones, especially the ones that make you feel seen, valued, and loved. Those experiences shaped my desire to create beautiful, memorable events that feel attainable and not overwhelming.

I believe everyone deserves a special day that reflects their story, regardless of budget. That belief is the foundation of my business. To help make high quality design more accessible, I provide my full decor inventory at no additional cost to my clients when they use my services.

Design that feels beautiful and attainable

This approach allows couples and families to enjoy an elevated, cohesive look while keeping services budget friendly and stress free. I offer this to support clients who are working within real budgets and still want their event to feel special and fully considered.

From intimate gatherings to full scale celebrations, I focus on thoughtful design, seamless coordination, and intentional details. I can assist with venue selection, vendor management, timelines, custom decor, tablescapes, balloons, florals, food, and full event design.

Why I do this

Celebrations matter. Milestones were not always guaranteed, which gave me a deep appreciation for the moments that bring people together. Those experiences shaped my heart for this work and inspired me to create events that feel full of love.

I want my clients to feel supported and excited, not stressed or overwhelmed by costs. This work is personal, and every event I design reflects my commitment to making people feel special, cared for, and celebrated.

A personal promise

Connection is at the center of every event.

Above all, I take the time to truly understand my clients so each event feels personal, meaningful, and effortless. Your milestones deserve to be celebrated with care, and I am honored to help bring your vision to life.
